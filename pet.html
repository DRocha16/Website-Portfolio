Voice Your Pet

Shattering Barriers Between Pets and Pet Lovers


This website was a work for a course on innovation. We were encouraged to come up with an idea for an startup. Our idea was to use Machine Learning methods to translate the barks of a dog into a need of the dog. The reasoning was that some types of barks might associated with some types of needs of the dog like hunger, tiredness or sickness.

So, for that we needed data to train the model, hence the first step would be to collect data from various dog owners. This website was a functional prototype of what it would be.

My part on it was fundamentally the frontend development.
